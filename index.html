Short Bio

I am a second-year master student at Tsinghua University advised by Prof. Xiu Li. Now I work closely with Dr. Kai Li and Dr. Yulun Zhang. Previously, I received my B.Eng. degree from Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications (NJUPT) under the supervision of Prof. Hu Zhu and Dr. Guoxia Xu.

My research interests revolve around the intersection of computer vision, biomedical image analysis, and machine learning. Specifically, I emphasize the utilization of prior knowledge to augment the robustness and generalization capabilities of computer vision algorithms.

I am looking for a PhD position in Fall, 2024. I'm open to any meaningful research topics during my doctoral studies.
