Short Bio

I am a second-year master student at Northwestern Polytechnical University (NWPU) advised by Prof. Feiping Nie. Previously, I received my B.Eng. degree from Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications (NJUPT) under the supervision of Prof. Lei Chen.

My research interests focus on Dimensionality Reduction, including feature selection and clustering. I am also interested in the graph machine learning and the uncertainty in machine learning.
